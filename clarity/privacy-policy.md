# Privacy Policy for Clarity

**Effective Date: December 28, 2025**

Welcome to Clarity. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Voice Data
Clarity uses the Feynman Technique to help you learn by explaining topics out loud. We require access to your device's microphone to process your explanations. 
- **Processing:** Your voice recordings are processed into text to provide feedback.
- **Storage:** We do not store raw audio files on our servers unless explicitly stated for specific features.

### B. Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage data such as features accessed, time spent in the app, and crash reports to improve the application's performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:
- Provide the core functionality of the app (AI-driven feedback).
- Improve our algorithms and user experience.
- Respond to support requests.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized data with third-party service providers (such as AI processing APIs) strictly for the purpose of app functionality.

## 4. Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.
