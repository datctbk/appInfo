## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. What is the Feynman Technique?
The Feynman Technique is a four-step process for understanding any topic:
1. Choose a concept.
2. Teach it to a child (explain it simply out loud).
3. Identify gaps in your explanation.
4. Review and simplify.

### 2. Why does the app need microphone access?
Clarity listens to your oral explanations to analyze your understanding and identify areas where you might need more review.

### 3. Is my data secure?
Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Please refer to our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md) for more details.
